ABOUT ME

BIOGRAPHY

Born in Burgos, Spain, she began studying violin
at the conservatory at the age of 7. In 2017 she
finished her intermediate grade of music
at the Rafael Frühbeck Conservatory in Burgos,
getting the honour degree for high grades.
That same year she began her violin superior
studies at the Conservatory of Castilla y León
(COSCyL) in Salamanca, where she, at the same time,
studied a philosophy degree at the University of
Salamanca (USAL). In those years she deepened her
knowledge not only of the violin, but also of music
theory, piano and jazz music. She finished her music
degree in 2022 with honours in the final project.
She is currently studying the last course of
philosophy in Turin, after being benefited from
an Erasmus scholarship.
